Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert

Rich Dad Poor Dad” is a 1997 book written by Robert T. Kiyosaki and Sharon Lechter. It advocates the importance of financial literacy, financial independence, and building wealth through investing in assets, real estate, and businesses. The book contrasts the financial mindsets of two father figures in Kiyosaki’s life: his “rich dad,” who accumulated wealth through entrepreneurship and savvy investing, and his “poor dad,” who worked hard but never achieved financial security
